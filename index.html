Clear View Homes LLC is a custom homebuilder serving the Northern Virginia area. Clear View Homes LLC builds all types of homes, from entry-level, single family homes, to exotic dream homes. Dedicated to quality, structural integrity and high efficiency construction, Clear View Homes LLC is experienced building with green materials and the use of alternate energies. While Clear View Homes LLC specializes in Arts & Craftsman style homes, we always build the home that suits your individual style.
