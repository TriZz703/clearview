Investing in the Future

With many roots in the Vienna area, John "JP" Sorrell was committed to bringing excellence into the diverse pool of local builders, when he opened the doors of Clear View Homes, LLC in 2005. He believes that quality workmanship should be the high standard homes are built to, not just putting one after another "Cookie Cutter" home up. To gain the knowledge and skill required of every level of construction JP apprenticed under other master craftsmen for many years, going from a Laborer to Carpenter to Forman to Superintendent. With all of that experience JP managed the construction of over 120 homes while with Michael Harris Homes, from breaking ground to handing over the key. Having worked in every facet of the business, JP felt it was time to part ways and bring his own keen vision, to the world of custom home building.

Since 1986 JP has expanded his familiarity and expertise with all aspects of home building to cultivate the brilliance in construction you deserve. It is his goal to provide steadfast, trustworthy & honest workmanship in every home he turns out. From hand carved millwork to artisan painting to landscaping, the dynamic team of craftsmen JP works with consistently meets and often exceeds the expectations of the client, with the superiority of their work.

New Homes

Many of the homes built by Clear View Homes, LLC are in the surrounding areas of Northern Virginia, The City of Falls Church, The Town of Vienna, and Oakton.

From concept drawing to walking over the threshold, Clear View Homes, LLC is there to guide the way. We have partnered with many amazing architects to bring forth the vision of the homeowner in a clear and concise way. We believe that homes should be built to last for generations to come. The high quality and remarkable craftsmanship we deliver are a testament to that.

Renovations & Additions

Clear View Homes, LLC not only constructs custom built homes but can also provide services for all-encompassing renovations and additions to your home.

Our renovation projects are beautifully turned out and marry with the current spaces skillfully. Every New build and renovation to a home is an investment in the future, not only in the family that will reside in the home but the community at large and Clear View Homes, LLC treats it as such. We believe that no detail should be left out and we endeavor to bring unparalleled service to every customer we work with.
